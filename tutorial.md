## gray 
- '0.95' 
- '0.75'
- '0.55' 
From 0 (darker) to 1 (lighter)

## using RBG tube (r,g,b)
(0, 0, 0) black
(1, 1, 1) white
(0.5, 0.8, 0.5) greener
(0.5, 0.5, 0.8) toward blue
(0.8, 0.5, 0.5) toward red
